📋 About Me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, advised by Prof. Lei Yang. Prior to that, I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2024, advised by Prof. Fan Wu. I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Northeastern University (China) in 2019.

My research focuses on mobile and ubiquitous sensing, with a current emphasis on optical signal-based applications.

If you are interested in collaborating or have any questions about my work, please do not hesitate to contact me at zimo_liao[at]outlook[dot]com.
